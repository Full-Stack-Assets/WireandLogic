Hetzner has announced a price adjustment for its dedicated servers and cloud plans, citing increased hardware procurement costs. The change affects new orders and rescales of existing servers, but does not impact existing server contracts or certain other products like web hosting and managed servers. This move is likely to have significant implications for Hetzner's customers and the broader cloud computing market.

<Callout type="takeaway">Hetzner's price adjustment reflects the challenges of hardware procurement in the IT industry.</Callout>

## What happened
Hetzner's price adjustment is part of a broader effort to standardize its dedicated server product structure. The company is introducing a new naming convention for its servers, with designations like "-1", "-2", and "-3" to indicate different configurations. Additionally, Hetzner is introducing a "limited" server type for products in limited quantities, which will be priced lower due to lower hardware costs.

The price adjustment affects all dedicated servers and cloud plans at all locations, but does not impact web hosting products, managed servers, servers from the Server Auction, IPs, storage products, Load Balancers, Volumes, Snapshots, and Object Storage. Existing server contracts will remain unchanged, but new orders and rescales of existing servers will be subject to the new pricing.

Hetzner has stated that the price adjustment is necessary due to the increased costs of operating its infrastructure and procuring new hardware. The company aims to provide stable and fair prices in the long term, despite the challenges posed by the current market.

## Why it matters
The price adjustment has significant implications for Hetzner's customers, who will need to factor in the increased costs when planning their infrastructure needs. The change may also impact the broader cloud computing market, as other providers may be facing similar challenges in terms of hardware procurement.

The introduction of the "limited" server type could provide customers with a more affordable option, but it may also create uncertainty around the availability of these servers. Hetzner's commitment to providing stable and fair prices in the long term will be crucial in maintaining customer trust and loyalty.
