A recent study from Pew Research has found that only 16% of Americans believe that AI will have a positive impact on society in the next 20 years. This comes as AI increasingly dominates our economy and daily lives. The study reveals that most Americans have neutral to negative views about AI, with around 40% saying that it will have a negative impact. <Callout type="takeaway">The majority of Americans are skeptical about the benefits of AI and its potential to improve society.</Callout> ## What happened The Pew Research study surveyed a wide range of Americans, including young people, adults, and seniors. The results show that young people, those under 30, are the most skeptical about AI, with only 14% believing it will have a positive impact. The study also found that a vast majority of Americans, nearly two-thirds, believe that AI's development is occurring too quickly. ## Why it matters The study's findings have significant implications for the development and implementation of AI in society. If most Americans are skeptical about the benefits of AI, it could impact the adoption and acceptance of AI technologies.
